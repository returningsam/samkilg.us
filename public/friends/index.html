Random Friends

As of 2018, there are over 1.5 billion websites. For any one person, this can be a frustrating thing when first entering their field. This is where networking becomes key.

Inspired by Nicolay Wesseltoft and friends, I decided to make this tool to make it easy for help your friends get the exposure we all deserve.

The process is simple: just add your friends' links in the area to the right and then hit "create link". Then, copy the link and add it to your website!

This service is 100% free to use and stores none of the information provided. While you're at it, why not check out my website!
