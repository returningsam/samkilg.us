Random Friends

As of 2018, there are well over 1.5 billion websites in existance. For any one person, this can be extremely frustrating when first entering their field. This is where networking becomes key.

Inspired by Nicolay Wesseltoft and friends, I made this tool to make it easy to help your friends get the exposure we all deserve.

The process is simple: just add your friends' links in the area to the right and then hit "create link". Then, copy the link and add it to your website!

This service is 100% free to use and stores none of the information provided. While you're at it, why not check out myself or one of my friends!
